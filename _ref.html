Core Thesis

Traditional software treats computation as expensive and data movement as free. Reality is inverted. Data movement costs 6400× more than compute.

I'm building systems where the physics of the domain becomes the type system. Making illegal states unrepresentable by encoding real-world constraints into the structure of the software itself.
